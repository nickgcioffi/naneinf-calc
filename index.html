What is this Balatro Naneinf Calculator?

This calculator estimates Balatro's score scaling for Baron and Mime builds without hitting JavaScript limits similar to Balatro's naneinf (~1.8e308).

It is a planning calculator, not a full Balatro engine. It focuses on key scaling factors such as total hand size, kings in hand, red seals, steel, Blueprint/Brainstorm and boss blinds effects (with the Serpent).

Other builds and jokers, like Perkeo with planet cards or cryptid cards, will be added in future updates.

What The App Counts

Baron-style scoring is modeled as repeated x1.5 multipliers from kings held in hand. Steel kings add more x1.5 triggers, and Mime-style retriggers repeat held-card effects. DNA and Burglar estimate how many extra kings you could build during a Serpent blind.

What is Naneinf?

Naneinf is a term in Balatro, for scores that exceed what the game can display. (around 1.8e308). At this point, the UI breaks and shows values like NaN (not a number) and inf (infinity), resulting in what you see in the UI: naneinf. Jokers like Baron, Mime, and Perkeo can help to achieve these explosive scores.
